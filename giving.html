Why Giving

We know that God is doing incredible things at Christ Apostolic Church Way and we believe it’s through the principle of Malachi 3.

“Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse, that there may be food in my house. Test me in this,” says the Lord Almighty, “and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven and pour out so much blessing that there will not be room enough to store it.” – Malachi 3:10 NIV”

As we bring the tithe we belive God will protect what we have, provide what we need, and pour blessing that we can’t contain. And that all starts with being obedient to His word. The more we trust Him, the more He entrusts in us. Below we have several easy and secure ways for you to tithe.
Thank you for giving generously and equipping us to serve our families and the communities around us.

Ways to Give

 Online Giving

This is a safe and easy way to simplify your giving by making a one-time contribution or establishing scheduled giving. You can even view your giving history when you create an account.

To give online just click here. You will be asked to create an account in FellowshipOne.

The secure service we use, Funraise, protects your privacy and security when you are donating online.

 Your Bank

Give through your bank’s online bill pay service. You can even set up recurring payments for your bank to mail to the church.

 Drop Off

Your giving can be dropped off at CAC Way Church offices Monday to Saturdays 8:30am-4:30pm. Our location is No.2 Olorunsogo, Challenge Area 2, Saki, Oyo State, Nigeria.

Gift Acceptance Policy

CAC Way is a registered not-for-profit 501(c)(3) corporation. All donations are tax deductible. No goods or services will  be provided in exchange for the contribution.
